FIELD GUIDANCE:

"product" / "price":
- EXACT product name including model number (e.g., "Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra" not just "Samsung phone")
- Price as mentioned by the reviewer, with currency

"rating":
- Use the reviewer's EXACT score and scale
- "score": The actual number given (e.g., 8.5)
- "maxScore": The scale maximum (usually 10, sometimes 5 or 100)
- "label": Short rating label matching the score (e.g., "Great", "Excellent", "Average")
- If no explicit score given, estimate based on overall tone: use 8+ for recommended, 5-7 for mixed, <5 for not recommended

"pros" (MINIMUM 3):
- SPECIFIC strengths, not generic praise
- Include numbers and details: "5000mAh battery lasts 2 full days" not just "good battery life"
- Order by importance (most significant pro first)

"cons" (MINIMUM 2):
- SPECIFIC weaknesses with details
- Include context: "No wireless charging, which competitors at this price offer" not just "no wireless charging"
- Be honest — even positive reviews have cons

"specs" (MINIMUM 3):
- Key specifications mentioned in the review
- Focus on specs the reviewer actually tested or discussed
- "key": Specification name (e.g., "Display", "Battery", "Weight", "Processor")
- "value": Exact spec value (e.g., "6.8-inch AMOLED, 120Hz", "5000mAh", "185g")
- Include measurements, model numbers, capacities — anything concrete

"comparisons":
- How this product compares to competitors
- "feature": The specific feature being compared
- "thisProduct": This product's spec/performance
- "competitor": Competitor's spec/performance
- "competitorName": EXACT competitor product name (e.g., "iPhone 15 Pro Max")
- Only include comparisons the reviewer actually makes

"verdict":
- "badge": One of: "recommended", "not_recommended", "conditional", "best_in_class"
  - "recommended": Overall positive, good value
  - "not_recommended": Too many issues, better alternatives exist
  - "conditional": Good for specific use cases only
  - "best_in_class": Top pick in its category
- "bestFor": Who should buy this (specific user types)
- "notFor": Who should NOT buy this (specific user types)
- "bottomLine": 1-2 sentence final verdict capturing the reviewer's conclusion
