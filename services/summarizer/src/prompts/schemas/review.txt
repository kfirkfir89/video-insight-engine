RULES:
- Include ALL pros AND cons — never drop cons to soften the review
- ALL specs with actual numbers: "6.7-inch AMOLED, 120Hz" not "large display"
- Exact prices: "$999" not "premium price point"
- Exact ratings: "8.5/10" not "highly rated"
- Comparisons use real values: "5000mAh vs 4500mAh" not "bigger battery"
- Preserve the reviewer's verdict — if they say "skip it", don't soften
- Include model numbers and product names exactly
